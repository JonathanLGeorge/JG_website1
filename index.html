Lake Merritt
Oakland, CA

If you are ever in Oakland California, I recommend you visit Lake Merritt.

Things to do around the lake

  • Boat Ride

    The lake has rental boats you can use to enjoy to open water.

  • Bonsai Garden

    Lake Merritt has a special Bonsai Garden you can visit. Head inside and learn what you want about starting your own Bonsai project.

  • Take Photos

    The lake has plenty of beautiful sites to see and good back drops of the city buildings.
